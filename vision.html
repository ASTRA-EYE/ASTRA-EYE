OUR VISION

A future built on real-time orbital intelligence

ASTRA’s vision is to become the first automated satellite and monitoring network operating across LEO, MEO, and GEO. Its mission is the automation of satellites.

LEO Orbit Buoys enables closer coverage, low latency and flexible deployment.

In the future, the goal is to deploy a network of floating buoys in LLO orbit around the Moon to monitor and automate the operations of space assets — satellites, space tugs and more — positioned in LEO, MEO, and GEO.

Building ASTRA-EYE is a key step toward lunar infrastructure: the technology and data gathered would directly support the planning and execution of projects on the lunar surface, enabling a safer and more efficient environment for construction on the Moon.
